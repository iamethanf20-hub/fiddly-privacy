Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I search for text in my photos?
A: Open Findly, make sure you're in OCR mode (the default), enter the text you're looking for, and tap "Find!" The app will scan your photos and highlight any matches.
Q: What's the difference between "Contains" and "Exact" match?
A: "Contains" finds photos with text that includes your search term (e.g., searching "cat" finds "catalog"). "Exact" only matches the complete word you entered.
Q: How many free searches do I get?
A: Free users get 15 searches per month. The quota resets on the first day of each month. Premium subscribers get unlimited searches.
Q: What is Object Detection (OWL mode)?
A: Object Detection is a premium beta feature that lets you search for physical objects in your photos (e.g., "mug," "laptop," "dog"). This feature requires a Findly Premium subscription.
Q: Are my photos uploaded to the cloud?
A: No! All image processing happens locally on your device. Your photos never leave your iPhone, and we don't have access to them.
Q: How do I manage my subscription?
A: Go to iPhone Settings â†’ [Your Name] â†’ Subscriptions â†’ Findly. You can view, change, or cancel your subscription there.
Q: How do I restore my purchase on a new device?
A: Your subscription is tied to your Apple ID. Simply sign in with the same Apple ID on your new device, and your premium features will be restored automatically.
Q: The app isn't finding text in my photo. Why?
A: Make sure the text in the photo is clear and legible. Blurry, rotated, or very small text may not be detected. Also try different match modes or languages if applicable.
Q: Can I get a refund?
A: Refunds are handled by Apple. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com and sign in with your Apple ID.
Q: What languages does OCR support?
A: Findly supports English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Chinese, Japanese, and Korean. Select your language from the language picker in the app.
