Privacy Policy

Introduction

Findly ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Findly collects minimal information to provide our text and object detection services:

  • Photos and Camera Access: We access your photos and camera only when you choose to use our text detection features. All image processing happens locally on your device. We do not store, transmit, or share your photos.
  • Usage Information: We track the number of searches you perform to enforce the free tier limit (15 searches per month for free users).
  • Purchase Information: If you subscribe to Findly Premium, Apple processes and stores your subscription information through the App Store.
  • Advertising Data (Free Users Only): We use Google AdMob to display advertisements to free users. AdMob may collect device identifiers, location data, and usage information for advertising purposes. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for more information about how AdMob handles data.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide text and object detection services
  • To track usage limits for free tier users
  • To process subscription payments (handled by Apple)
  • To display relevant advertisements to free users (handled by AdMob)

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Apple: Subscription and payment information is processed through Apple's App Store
  • Google AdMob: Advertising data for free users is shared with AdMob for ad personalization
  • Object Detection Service: When you use the OWL (object detection) feature, images are processed through our third-party object detection service. Images are not stored after processing.

Your Choices

  • Camera and Photos: You can revoke camera and photo access at any time in your device Settings
  • Advertising Tracking: You can limit ad tracking through your device's privacy settings
  • Premium Subscription: Subscribe to Findly Premium to remove ads and gain unlimited searches

Data Security

All image processing happens locally on your device. We do not store your photos on our servers. Usage and subscription data is protected using industry-standard security measures.

Children's Privacy

Findly is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
