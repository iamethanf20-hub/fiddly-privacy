Privacy Policy for Findly

Last Updated: October 29, 2025

Overview

Findly is an image search application that helps you find text and objects in photos. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handle your data with care.

Information We Collect

1. Photos and Camera Access

Findly requires access to your camera and photo library to analyze images. All image processing happens locally on your device. We do not upload, store, or transmit your images to any external servers.

2. Usage Data

We store your monthly search count locally on your device to enforce the free tier limit of 15 searches per month. This data never leaves your device and is used solely for quota management.

3. Subscription Information

If you purchase Findly Premium, your subscription is managed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We receive only your subscription status (active or expired) from Apple. We do not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or other financial data.

For more information about how Apple handles your data, see Apple's Privacy Policy.

4. Advertising Data (Free Users Only)

The free version of Findly displays advertisements powered by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect the following data:

  • Approximate location (for ad targeting)
  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID/IDFA)
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)
  • Device information (type, operating system version)

This data is collected and managed by Google, not by Findly. Premium subscribers do not see ads and this data is not collected for them.

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

How We Use Your Information

  • Photos: Processed locally on your device for text and object detection
  • Usage Data: To manage and enforce free tier quotas
  • Subscription Status: To unlock premium features for subscribers
  • Advertising Data: Collected by AdMob to display relevant ads (free users only)

Data Storage and Security

All data collected by Findly is stored locally on your device. We do not use cloud storage or maintain external servers for user data. Your images are never uploaded or transmitted outside your device.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. The only data sharing that occurs is:

  • Subscription status shared with Apple (for managing your subscription)
  • Advertising data collected by Google AdMob (for free users only)

Your Rights and Choices

  • Delete Data: You can delete all local data by uninstalling the app
  • Manage Subscription: Control your subscription through iOS Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions
  • Ad Tracking: Control ad tracking through iOS Settings > Privacy > Tracking
  • Remove Ads: Subscribe to Findly Premium to remove all ads and associated data collection

Children's Privacy

Findly is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us to have it removed.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
